Typography Standards
01. Fraunces (Mincho Emulation)
Ink Bleed Outlier Outcomes.

Variables: SOFT 100, WONK 1, OPSZ 144. Used for narrative gravity and philosophical anchors. The extreme softness emulates Sumi ink bleeding into paper fibers.

02. Inter (NASA 1976 Spec)
Medium / For Emphasis
Light / For Informational Clarity

Following the 1976 NASA Graphics Standards, we use Inter Light (300) for body and Inter Medium (500) for labels. This ensures maximum legibility and objective authority.
