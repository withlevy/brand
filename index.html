05 // TOGGLES

ACCORDIONS (FAQ)

What is the Levy Studio?

The portfolio and program-building arm. Designing, launching, and scaling initiatives that demonstrate what cross-sector coalition building actually produces.

How does the community capital framework apply?

Durable outcomes require simultaneous investment across the forms of capital a community runs on — human, social, cultural, financial, built, and political.

Is Levy a political organization?

No. Levy operates as a builder, working in the places and with the people where the promise of the American Project is most actively being tested. It is strictly non-partisan.
